Innovation

Infrastructure

His innovative approach to lending small amounts of money to poor individuals, particularly women, has helped millions escape poverty. Dr. Yunus was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006 for his work in creating economic and social development from below, demonstrating that financial services could empower the poorest to transform their lives."
